Borrowing Guide Lines                                                            
                   Library appreciate cash donations of any amount.

                   Your monetary contribution to the Library will help us buy new books and media for our libraries. Large and small donations help BPL offset budget cuts to continue growing our valuable collection. Donate today.

                   To make a monetary donation please use our donation form.

                   Donors receive an "Acceptance of Materials" form. We are unable to appraise donated materials.

                   We accept donations at any library location during open hours.
