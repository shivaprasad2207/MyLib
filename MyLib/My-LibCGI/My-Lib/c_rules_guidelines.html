Guide Lines and Procedures                                                            
     Noise
                   Please do not indulge in loud conversations that are disruptive to other students. Quiet conversations are allowed, as long as other students are not disturbed. In the quiet study area of the library even quiet conversation should be avoided.

     Food and Beverages
                   Food and beverages are allowed in the library. Please keep your food and beverages in covered containers to avoid spillage.
We encourage “neat” snacks.

     Children in the Library
                   Supervised children are allowed in the library as long as they are not disturbing the other patrons. If a child is being disruptive, the librarian on duty will approach the supervising adult and address the problem
